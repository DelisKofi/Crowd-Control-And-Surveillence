Get to know more about AI Powered overcrowding control systems

In today's data-driven world there is the potential to use controllers that can store large amounts of data which is not possible with with traditional controller. Our goal is to propose an artificial intelligence based controller system for overcrowded area that maintain data integrity,saves memory, reduces computation and simplifies program design.

AI has different controllers for their security

Face detection

Face detection technology can be used in a variety of field including security, biometrics,law enforcement and personal safety to provide real-time surveillence and tracking of people

People Counting

In order to accurately count the number of people in a crowd in real-time, crowd counting technology use AI to detect and count the number of heads

Emotions

Using facial expressions and physiological cues, such as heart rates and brain actitvities, emotion detection technology, a subset of AI tries to determine how a human subject is feeling. This software can also monitor eye movement to determine which stimuli the subject is focussing on most

Intelligent surveillence

  • Unique IDs

    The system automatically assigns each person under cover with a unique identification number that can be used to track and identify the person

  • Detection and Analysis of Emotions

    Unlike many crowd monitoring systems, our proposed system detects and analysis the person's emotions

  • People Counting

    The P-Tech software counts the number of people inside the area under suiveillance.
